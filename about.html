About us

Our Qualified Team is Ready to Help You!

NEU's Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Internet of Things (IoT) spans several computing disciplines in: machine learning and cognitive next-generation networks ruggedized for challenging conditions, Human-to-Machine (H2M) interfaces, 6G and beyond communications, intelligent multimedia services, smart spaces and the Internet of Everything (IoE). We participate in sketching future life trends, and we assure intelligence in everything through innovative solutions and ideas. We are located within the Engineering Faculty, at Near East University (NEU) in Nicosia, Cyprus and we have research partners and collaborators worldwide. We provide outstanding consultancy and technical research services in the area.
